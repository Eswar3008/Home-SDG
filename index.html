About SDG Campus Club

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals are the Blueprint to achieve a better and more Sustainable Future for all. The inception of the SDG Campus club which was based on the Theme of promoting the 17 SDGs of the UN and conducting events/seminars for raising awareness on campus, encompasses all aspects of Societal Development including – Alleviating Poverty, Promoting Healthy Lifestyle, Promoting Sustainable use of Energy and maintaining Proper Hygiene, Women Empowerment, Education for all etc.

SDG CC NITRKL

SDG Campus Club is the Official Sustainability Club of NIT Rourkela and regularly organizes events in collaboration with other Social Service clubs. Feel free to reach out to us for collaborations and partnerships at sdgcampusclub.nitrkl@gmail.com
